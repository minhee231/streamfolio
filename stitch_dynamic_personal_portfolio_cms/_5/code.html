The Challenge

The architectural sector faced a significant bottleneck in real-time rendering and client collaboration. Traditional static renders failed to convey spatial dynamics, leading to 35% more revision cycles than necessary. We needed to create a system that allowed for sub-millisecond ray tracing on mobile devices while maintaining 4K fidelity for presentation.

The Process

Our sprint began with a comprehensive audit of existing WebGL pipelines. We implemented a custom shader library designed to offload 40% of geometry processing to the client GPU. Over 4 months, we prototyped three distinct UI paradigms—asymmetric, bento, and traditional—ultimately landing on the cinematic immersive mode seen here.
